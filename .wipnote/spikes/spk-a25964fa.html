The check-host-paths pre-commit gate only scans staged files. Investigate whether host-local absolute paths (workspaces, home, Users, tmp, private/var prefixes) exist elsewhere: already-committed files, gitignored files that ship in other ways (templates, embedded assets), allowlist contents, scripts, tests. Produce categorized findings: (a) violations in committed tracked files, (b) allowlist entries and whether each is legitimate, (c) assets embedded in the Go binary that might carry paths, (d) any other leak surfaces.
