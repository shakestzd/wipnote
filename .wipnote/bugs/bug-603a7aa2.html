Description

Roborev #1502 (1 MED): internal/otel/sink/sqlite/queued.go:60 — queued closure captures resourceAttrs and signals by reference and runs AFTER WriteBatch returns. Callers that reuse or mutate the slice, signal structs, or attribute maps after the call can race with the queue worker or persist corrupted/stale data. Fix: snapshot the batch before submitting — copy the signals slice; clone mutable maps/fields such as resourceAttrs, RawAttrs, and Success so the queued op owns its inputs.
